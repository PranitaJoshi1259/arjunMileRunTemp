2.5 All Participants and Participant Teams must comply with all race rules, which include, but are not limited to, those listed below:

    2.6.1 TEAM ELIGIBILITY
  1. Each team should consist of 10 participants, but the minimum number of participants per team is 8. In this situation, Any 2 participants must run another lap each in order for the team to complete all 10 laps. No participant is permitted to run more than 2 laps.
  2. Participants are permitted to run in only one team at any given race and must be at least 18 years of age on the event date.
  3. A minimum of 8 individual participants in each team must be employed by the corporation or be a member of the professional organisation entering the event – verification of eligibility must be provided upon request.
  4. If a corporate/organisation has less than 20 employees or members, they are able to group together with one other corporate/organisation that also has less than 20 employees or members in order to form a team, but at least half of the participants in the team must be employees or members from the registered corporation.
  5. Maximum 2 organisation can form a team. More than 2 organisations in one team are not allowed.
  6. There are 3 different team categories in the race: Overall, Mixed and Female. Team Eligibility Rules 1-5 apply to all 3 categories.
  7. All teams will be eligible for the Overall category by default.
  8. To be eligible for the Mixed category, A team will need to maintain 4:6 ratio, running a minimum of one lap each.
  9. To be eligible for the Female category, a team will need to consist of female participants only.
  10. The fastest Overall team, the fastest Mixed team and the fastest Female team will be awarded after the event has finished, but if the event needs to be stopped for any reason the awards will only be presented to teams that have completed at least 5 laps.
  11. Minimum requirement for the awards is minimum 3 teams competing in each category (Overall/Mixed/Female)

    12. 2.6.2 RACE RULES
  12. Every team must complete 10 laps of the designated one-mile (1.6km) course.
  13. There can only be one participant per team on the course at any given time.
  14. Should any participant wish to walk instead of run during their lap, always keep to the side of the course to prevent blocking other participants.
  15. Your race baton contains your timing chip. Participants can only pass the baton by hand to their own team members in the transition area opposite the race pens - throwing, rolling or kicking the baton is not allowed.
  16. After handing the baton to their team member, the participant must immediately move into the Finish Area. The last participant must return their team baton to staff in the finish area. Loss/Failure to submit the baton to the staff will cost you 2000/- INR which will be deducted from the security deposit.
  17. Each participant is required to display their bib number clearly throughout the race on the front of their t-shirt or vest.
  18. You must treat every participant, volunteer, spectator, and staff member with respect regardless of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, age, ability, or any other characteristic. Disrespectful behaviour of any kind will not be tolerated.
  19. Always prioritise the safety of yourself and others. Routes can be narrow in places so please ensure you follow the instructions of event staff, be aware of your surroundings, and give each other space wherever possible to prevent accidents and injuries.
  20. If there is reason to believe that a lap time has been achieved by unfair means, that lap may be subject to a time penalty at the Race Organiser's discretion.
    21. No appeals are allowed. The Race Organiser's decision is final.
    All company teams acknowledge that company entries which do not qualify under these rules, whether or not they have been accepted initially by the Race Organizer, may be rejected by the Race Organiser at their discretion before the race and their race entry fee will be refunded in full.
